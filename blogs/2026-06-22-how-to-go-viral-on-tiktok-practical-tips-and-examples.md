Going viral on TikTok comes from understanding the algorithm, which prioritizes watch time, engagement, shares, and saves over follower count, rather than chasing trends randomly. The guide covers clear audience targeting, strong hooks in the first 3 seconds, strategic trend use, focused short videos, and engagement-driving content, citing brands like Duolingo, Ryanair, and Red Bull as examples of distinctive styles that outperform direct product promotion.
